# Project Name Ideas

Alternatives to "Dialectic" (which is trademarked by multiple companies including Dialectic AG, a Swiss crypto hedge fund).

## Available Names

| Name | Meaning | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| **Elenchus** | Socratic cross-examination method | Best fit - captures competitive debate nature |
| **Aporia** | State of intellectual puzzlement | Evokes the challenge of debate |
| **Parrhesia** | Bold speech, fearless debate | Greek concept of frank discourse |
| **Dissoi Logoi** | "Double arguments" (Pro vs Con) | Ancient Greek debate format |
| **Agora** | Greek public assembly/debate square | Some existing uses, but not in AI/gaming |

## Recommendations

1. **Elenchus** - Top pick. The Socratic elenchus is a method of refutation through cross-examination, perfectly matching the AI bot debate format.

2. **Aporia** - Strong second choice. Represents the intellectual impasse that good debates create.

3. **Parrhesia** - Bold and distinctive. Means "to speak freely" - fits the uncensored AI debate concept.
