# Project Name Ideas

Alternatives to "Dialectic" (which is trademarked by multiple companies including Dialectic AG, a Swiss crypto hedge fund).

## Available Names

| Name | Meaning | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| **Elenchus** | Socratic cross-examination method | Best fit - captures competitive debate nature |
| **Aporia** | State of intellectual puzzlement | Evokes the challenge of debate |
| **Parrhesia** | Bold speech, fearless debate | Greek concept of frank discourse |
| **Dissoi Logoi** | "Double arguments" (Pro vs Con) | Ancient Greek debate format |
| **Agora** | Greek public assembly/debate square | Some existing uses, but not in AI/gaming |

## Recommendations

### Tier 1 - Top Picks
1. **Agon** - Short, memorable, easy to say/type. Meaning (contest/struggle) is perfect for competitive debate.
2. **Eris** - Edgy, memorable. Goddess of discord. 4 letters. Has personality.
3. **Kairos** - Sophisticated. "The right moment" - captures the timing aspect of debate.

### Tier 2 - Strong Alternatives
4. **Logos** - Well-known, credible. Aristotle's mode of persuasion through reason.
5. **Elenchus** - Socratic cross-examination method. Academic but distinctive.
6. **Nemesis** - Well-known, competitive edge. Goddess of retribution.

### Tier 3 - Unique Options
7. **Doxa** - Very short (4 letters), means "opinion" - what debates are about.
8. **Polemos** - "War/conflict" - aggressive, root of "polemic".
9. **Metis** - Cunning intelligence. Unusual but memorable.

### Wild Cards
- **Eristic** - Art of disputation. Sounds techy.
- **Tyche** - Fits the betting/luck aspect.
- **Athlon** - Contest/prize. Sporty vibe.
